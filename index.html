👍 So, What is mining?

Mining is a process that takes place on the blockchain with every peaceful action you perform. Each action is recorded and visible to all users connected to the blockchain. All actions are tallied and linked to a wallet: yours.

You don't need a machine or electricity.

All you need is your phone and your willingness to participate.

If you reach higher levels → you can mine faster.

👍 Does everyone earn the same amount?

Yes and no.
The rules of the game are the same for everyone, but if some have many friends or invest in NFTs to progress faster, then they will earn significantly more tokens.

👍 Every 30 days, the mining reward will be halved.

This system ensures fairness for everyone and rewards your actions: it's called the halving.

If rewards remained high indefinitely, the system would be flooded with tokens and their value would decrease significantly. The halving slows production over time, preserves scarcity, controls inflation, and rewards early participants, while ensuring economic stability for everyone.

My name is Alan August, I am an entrepreneur, globetrotter, life lover and world citizen. Witnessing over the years all these suffering, I asked to myself and to my friends: How, in 2025, cannot we live in peace, how ?

NAVA PEACE is my answer and my humble invitation, to change the world.

"Guys, I am just a random dude dropped here 50 years ago by accident. My dude Steeve shared with me his pain about the suffering of Palestinian kids, I think it was the beginning of my deep reflection "and me what do I do, how can I help?".

A few months later, I spent 25 days in a Buddhist temple in Chiang Mai, in northern Thailand. I went there to find myself, to reconnect with the "me" that had been lost over the years, buried under the noise and chaos of life. A Vipassana, a silent retreat, is a journey inward. A way to return to oneself. A way to find peace, to reset, and to shine again. One of those days, I received this vision, an inspiration that came to me with clarity. We cannot continue this way...

PEACE IS THE ONLY WAY.

There is not better investment than a world at peace.

With NAVA PEACE App each tap is a whisper of peace in becoming. Millions of whispers become a voice the world cannot ignore.

Alan August
Founder, NAVA PEACE
